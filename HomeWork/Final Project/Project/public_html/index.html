Bangladesh win the toss and elect to bowl

Dehra Dun, India: Bangladesh have won the toss and they decided to bowl first. The pitch in the practice match was low and slow, and a little two-paced. This one feels less soft, and they are hoping for better value for strokes. Afghanistan: Mohammad Shahzad(w), Usman Ghani, Asghar Stanikzai(c), Shafiqullah Shafiq, Najibullah Zadran, Mohammad Nabi, Samiullah Shenwari, Rashid Khan, Mujeeb Ur Rahman, Karim Janat, Shapoor Zadran. Bangladesh: Tamim Iqbal, Liton Das, Shakib Al Hasan(c), Mushfiqur Rahim(w), Mahmudullah, Sabbir Rahman, Mosaddek Hossain, Rubel Hossain, Nazmul Islam, Abul Hasan, Abu Jayed. This will be only the second T20I between the two teams, with Bangladesh winning their only match during the 2014 World Twenty20 in Dhaka. This time however, Bangladesh come into the match as slight underdogs as they are ranked two places below Afghanistan at 10th place in the ICC T20I rankings. Moreover, they were handily beaten by eight wickets in Friday's practice game between Bangladesh and an Afghan XI, which contained many of the players that will feature in Afghanistan's XI today.
